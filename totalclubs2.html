About Coding Club

oding Club YCCE (also known as CodeChef YCCE Chapter) is responsible for participating and organizing activities in the field of competitive coding. The activities include coding competitions, webinars and collaborating with other colleges to promote a healthy coding environment. Coding club highly values problem solving and critical thinking skills. Coding Club aims to establish a coding culture on campus, reaching every student passionate about coding. In a coding club, you’ll be able to collaborate with others, learn from them and indeed teach them things you know — all in an environment far removed from the pressures of academic study.
